# MovieMania

## A Movie mobile application

The MovieMania Android App is designed to help users plan their movie-watching efficiently, manage their watchlists, and track their movie preferences. Whether you're trying to discover new movies, save money on rentals/tickets, or simply organize your movie life better, this app is here to assist you.

## Key Features

- *Movie Details*: View detailed information about each Movie, including description, rating, and videos.
- *Favorites*: Add Movie recipes to your favorites for easy access.
- *Search Functionality*: Search for meals by name, category, ingredient, or country.
